Entry of the Week · Reviewed by the Standards Board

Yetis will not accept a sandwich with the cheese laid flat.

The cheese must be folded. A flat slice reads, in most alpine yeti communities, as a deliberate provocation — and three field teams have the ruined coolers to prove it.
